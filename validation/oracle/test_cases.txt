# Vegas Blitz Deterministic Core Oracle Cases

Notation:
- Windows are 6 reels x 4 rows.
- Payout expectations are in paytable units unless `xBet` or `total bet` is stated.
- `COR(10x)` means a Coin symbol carrying a 10x total-bet value.
- These cases are simulator-core cases only; UI, buttons, sounds, logs, popups, and server forcing are out of scope.

row 0 | HV1 | HV2 | HV3 | HV4 | HV5 | LV1

row 2 | HV2 | HV3 | HV4 | HV5 | LV1 | LV2
row 3 | LV3 | LV4 | LV5 | LV6 | HV1 | HV2

EXPECT:
- ways_win = 0
- scatter_win = 0
- collect_win = 0
- feature_trigger = none

## TC-FS-001 Free Spins WILD Multiplier Product

## TC-BLITZ-001 Blitz Collect Guarantee And Collection

MODE: BLITZ_SPINS
BET_FIXED_COINS: 20

FULL_WINDOW:
row 0 | COLLECT | COR(1x) | COR(4x) | COR(5x) | COR(10x) | COLLECT
row 1 | COR(2x) | COR(3x) | COR(1x) | COR(2x) | COR(1x)  | COR(5x)
row 2 | COR(1x) | COR(2x) | COR(3x) | COR(4x) | COR(5x)  | COR(6x)
row 3 | COR(2x) | COR(1x) | COR(2x) | COR(1x) | COR(2x)  | COR(1x)

EXPECT:
- symbols_allowed = COR, jackpot-valued COR, COLLECT only
- COLLECT count = 2
- at least one COR present = true
- collected COR total excluding COLLECT cells = 64x
- collect_win = 2 x 64 x total bet = 2560
- retrigger = none

## TC-BUY-001 Buy Bonus Option Mapping

MODE: BUY_BONUS_CONFIG

EXPECT:
- 4 SCAT option cost = 80x game bet; awards 12 Free Spins or 4 Blitz Spins.
- 5 SCAT option cost = 95x game bet; awards 20 Free Spins or 6 Blitz Spins.
- 6 SCAT option cost = 110x game bet; awards 30 Free Spins or 8 Blitz Spins.
- Buy Bonus availability is disabled in UKGC-facing scenarios and enabled only when jurisdiction/config permits it.

NOTE:
- QA workbook rows 403-405 say "6 Scatter symbols should land" for all Buy Bonus options; rows 411 and 414-425 support 4/5/6 SCAT by option. Do not silently resolve this in code.

MODE: BASE_ADDITIONAL_COR

PRE_TRIGGER_WINDOW:
row 0 | COLLECT | WILD | SCAT | HV4 | HV5 | LV1
row 1 | LV2     | LV3  | LV4  | LV5 | LV6 | HV1

EXPECT:
